What is OCR Technology?

OCR stands for Optical Character Recognition. It's a technology that can "read" text from images, converting visual text into digital text that computers can understand and process. RizzGPT uses advanced OCR to read the text from your dating app screenshots, understand the conversation context, and suggest appropriate replies.
