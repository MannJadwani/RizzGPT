More Gen Z Dating Slang You Should Know

Situationship

A casual relationship that's more than friends but less than a committed relationship. It's that gray area where you're dating but not officially together.

Ghosting

When someone suddenly stops responding to your messages without explanation. They just disappear from your life (and your DMs).

DM Slide

Sliding into someone's DMs (direct messages) ‚Äì usually on Instagram or Twitter. It's the modern way of making the first move.

Cuffing Season

The time of year (usually fall/winter) when people want to be in a relationship. It's when you want someone to "cuff" (commit to) you.

Lowkey / Highkey

Lowkey = secretly or quietly ("I'm lowkey into them"). Highkey = openly or obviously ("I highkey need to work on my rizz").

Simp

Someone who does too much for someone they're attracted to, often without getting anything in return. It's about being overly eager.

Beige Flags

Boring or slightly off-putting traits that aren't deal-breakers but make you go "hmm." Not red flags, just... beige.

Ick
